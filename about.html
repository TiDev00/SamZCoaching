Founder and Managing Director

Biography

El Hadj Danfa, a trusted advisor, certified career and executive coach with dual master's degrees in international economics and business administration, has cultivated a rich tapestry of expertise from prestigious institutions like the University Pierre Mendes France and the University of Science and Technology of Lille in France, as well as La Sorbonne. He also holds a certificate in financial planning from the University of Quebec and a securities course from the Canadian Securities Institute

His journey encompasses diverse leadership roles, as Chief Import Compliance Operations at Natural Resources Canada and Vice Chair of the Canadian Advisory Council on Energy Efficiency. With a 15- year tenure in the Canadian federal public service, El Hadj's strategic acumen and deep knowledge of public sector management were showcased in key positions such as Deputy Director of Planning, Performance, and Knowledge Management at Foreign Affairs Canada and Project Director for the Haiti Tax Mobilization Support Project.

Beyond borders, El Hadj's global impact includes a role as a consultant for the World Bank in Haiti, collaborating with international bodies like USAID, World Bank, IMF, European Union, and IADB. He also worked as a financial advisor and credit specialist at several Canadian financial institutions including Desjardins and the Royal Bank of Canada. His experiences in the Middle East, Haiti, Colombia, Afghanistan, and Pakistan have not only expanded his global perspective but also equipped him with tactical skills to navigate complex economic, political, and financial terrains.

Today, as a trusted advisor, executive coach and master facilitator from the Center for Executive Coaching in Florida, El Hadj channels his wealth of experience into shaping leaders. His approach, rooted in astute project management, strategic planning, and profound cross-cultural understanding, defines him as a leader who forges alliances, fosters growth, and guides organizations to unparalleled success.

On his free times El Hadj loves to travel and discover new places and cultures (visited more than 30 countries in 4 continents). He is a Karate black belt and an instructor for kids.
